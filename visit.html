General NYC Visitor Information

A live Customer Care Representative at the Welcome Center in Times Square can provide information and assist you in planning the perfect trip to the Brooklyn Bridge with lots of maps and information.

Wedding Planning

Some couples arrive in NYC and decide that they want to marry with the backdrop of the bridge. Should you wish to do the same, apply for a wedding permit.
